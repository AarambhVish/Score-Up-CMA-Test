Copy Paste Questions

Paste numbered MCQs. The app will preserve maths symbols, fractions, account tables, and economics diagram notes as far as the copied text allows.

Use Answer: A, Ans. B, or leave answer blank for later review. For visuals, paste a line like Diagram: demand curve shifts right or Table: Trial balance data below. For Economics diagrams, paste the diagram screenshot in the image box so the shape remains intact. Tables copied with tabs, pipes, or aligned columns will be stored with line breaks. Past paper answer keys should be pasted from a verified source; unverified answers stay pending.
